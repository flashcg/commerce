### Q: I happen to some problems near the end of the DVD.

A:
Please try the instructions below:
1. Firstly, please select "D9 to D5 movie only disc copy" and backup your DVD on your hard disc , then play it with PowerDVD or WinDVD. Please test if you get the same problem.
2. Please change the D5 Disc Size to 4100 or 4200, which is in the Advanced Setup in the expert interface. Then try again.
